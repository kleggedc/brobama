Ex-President Barack Obama

The Man Who Made History

Ex-President Barack Obama. The man who made history. 44th President of the United States

Interesting facts about Barack Obama:

  • Born on August 4, 1961, in Honolulu, Hawaii.
  • Obama attended Occidental College in Los Angeles in 1979. After two years, he transferred to New York's Columbia University, majored in political science and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in 1983
  • While at Harvard Law, he was the first ever African-American president of the Harvard Law Review
  • After graduation from Harvard Law in 1991, Obama taught constitutional law from 1992 to 2004 at the University of Chicago Law School
  • Wrote an autobiography titled "Dreams from My Father" in 1995.
  • Obama's mother died in Honolulu in 1995
  • Obama and Michelle married in October of 1993.
  • Time Magazine named him “Person of the Year” for 2008. He won this title again in 2012
  • On January 20, 2009 Obama was sworn in as the 44th president of the United States. He was 47 years old, the fifth youngest person to become president. His inauguration was the largest attendance ever seen for any event in Washington, D.C. with nearly 2 million people attending.
  • He was re-elected on November 6, 2012 and inaugurated on January 20, 2013 as required by the Constitution in a private ceremony in the White House(as it was a Sunday). The public ceremony at the capital occurred the next day.
  • Barack Obama left office on January 20, 2017 after two terms as president. His presidential library will be built in Chicago to house his papers and memorabilia.
